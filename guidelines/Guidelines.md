The Palette:
Deep Onyx (#0A0A0A): Your primary background.
Electric Neon Blue (#00F0FF): For CTA buttons and data highlights.
Stark White (#FFFFFF): For heavy headers.

The Texture: Apply a 15% opacity film grain overlay and Halftone patterns on background images of Pogradec to give it that "Grungy/Inverted" aesthetic.

The Typography:
Headlines: Heavyweight Grotesque (e.g., Founders Grotesk or Inter Tight Black).

Data/Technical: Clean Monospace (e.g., JetBrains Mono) for the budget and ROI figures.
